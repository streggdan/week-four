Today we are going to focus on what is in peanut butter

Peanut butter is made from ground peanuts that have been roasted. Sugar, salt (sodium), oils (fat) and additives that prevent separation may also be added

Below are the steps to make peanut butter

  • Put peanuts in a food processor
  • After 4 or 5 minutes stop food processor
  • Stir in a bit of honey to sweeten
  • Add salt if needed
  • Store in fridge
